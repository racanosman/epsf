Indonesia Festival 2017

Moscow, Russia

The Russian Pencak Silat Federation took part in the 2017 Indonesia Festival which was held in Moscow, Russia. The event was held to promote Indonesian culture, of which Silat is an important aspect. The Russian Pencak Silat Federation did all the Pencak Silat performances, demonstrations and Seminars.

Mr Valeriy Maistrovoy, President of Russian P.S. Federation/Vice President of EPSF, had a meeting at the Embassy with the Indonesian Ambassador to Russia HE Mr Wahid Supriyadi.

Coverage on Pencak Silat developments in Russia and achievements of the Russian Pencak Silat Federation in Indonesian newspaper Tribune News.
