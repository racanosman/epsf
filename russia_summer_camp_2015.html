Russian Pencak Silat Federation

hosts Silat Summer Camp

Sochi, Russia

The Russian Pencak Silat Federation (RPSF) recently hosted a Silat summer camp in Sochi, Russia which was attended by over 200 pesilat's from Eastern Europe. The camp was concluded with a tanding tournament attended by teams from Russia, Latvia, Azerbaijan, Slovakia, Lithuania and Krygyzstan.
